A landmark 200 year old Bar in the scenic seaside village of Rosses Point in Sligo, on the North West Coast of the ‘Wild Atlantic Way’.

Find Out More


With views of Sligo Bay, Oyster & Coney Islands and Knocknarea. casual dining a la carte in the evenings with daily specials.
”There are no strangers here only friends you have yet to meet” WB Yeats

There has been a pub on the site of Austies since the 17th Century. It is a seafaring bar with plenty of memorabilia and unique features, enjoy a drink at the Captain's Table, sit and look out onto ‘Memory Harbour’.
